The Story

Plagued by terrifying visions and a fractured reality, a paranoid schizophrenic breaks free from the sterile confines of her mental ward. Searching for sanctuary, she stumbles upon an abandoned, decaying Victorian house shrouded in fog.

But the walls hold secrets darker than her own mind. As the line between hallucination and the supernatural blurs, she must survive the night in a place where her worst fears are no longer just in her head.
